About Us

TechTogether New York is one of the few gender-focused hackathons based out of New York serving people of marginalized genders. Our mission is to empower all people of marginalized genders by providing opportunities to gain valuable skills and explore tech in an inclusive, inspiring, and engaging environment.
Our attendees are primarily high school, college, and bootcamp students in the tri-state area.

A hackathon is an event where individuals or teams work on technical projects in a short period of time (24-36 hours). With the help of workshops and mentors you can build an app, a website, a robot ‚Äî the possibilities are endless!

Our hackathons are very beginner friendly! We have intro workshops tailored to first-time hackers and a unique high school program to help assimilate all our hackers!

Your project will be submitted through Devpost. Click on the "Submit Project" button at the top of the site to access the Devpost.
Your submission must include a video (no longer than 2 minutes) that showcases your project, a basic description of your project, and the challenge(s) for which you want your project to be considered.

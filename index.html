About Us

TechTogether New York is one of the few gender-focused hackathons based out of New York serving people of marginalized genders. Our mission is to empower all people of marginalzied genders by providing opportunities to gain valuable skills and explore tech in an inclusive, inspiring, and engaging environment.
Our attendees are primarily high school, college, and bootcamp students in the tri-state area.
